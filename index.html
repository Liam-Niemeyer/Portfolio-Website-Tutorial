About Me

Let's tell stories that matter.

Hey. I'm an audio journalist based in western Kentucky at WKMS Public Radio, and also report for the Ohio Valley Resource, a multi-state (Ohio, Kentucky, West Virginia) news collaboration reporting in-depth stories about the region. Reach out with any questions, ideas, or otherwise.

What I Do

  • Community Theatre, when I have the time.

My Work

Here's a selection of my favorite pieces:

Rising Waters: Aging Levees, Climate Change And The Challenge To Hold Back The Ohio River

This story took a look at detoriating flood protections in the Ohio River Basin, and how it collides with the growing threat of climate change.

Sherman vs. Lee: How One Man Sparked A Fight To Take Down A Confederate Statue In His Kentucky Town

The story of a Black man -- a veteran, attorney, and a father of two young kids -- who sparked calls to remove a Confederate monument in a rural college town he only recently moved into to pursue his passion for coaching football.

The Long Wait: As Lawmakers Prioritize Broadband, Rural Communities Wonder When It Will Reach Them

The struggles of building rural broadband, exemplefied with one man and his isolated hometown.

A Blackface Photo Resurfaces, And A Kentucky Town Confronts Its Racial Trauma

Black commmunity members in a small Kentucky city deal with pain and trauma after a blackface photo resurfaces of their local superintendent.

Son carries on father's legacy at the Smiling Skull

With his father ill from cancer, a son carries on the legacy of his father's "biker bar" in Athens, Ohio.
