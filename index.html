Storytelling is my trade. On here, you'll find a collection of my journalistic work in radio, podcasting, print and more.

A quick summary of my experience: currently, I'm working as a summer news intern producing radio spots and features at KRBD Community Radio, the NPR affiliate of Ketchikan, Alaska. I recently graduated from Ohio University's E.W. Scripps School of Journalism with a B.S. in Journalism and also launched a narrative podcast series called Timestop, documenting stories of life-changing moments in people's lives.

In the past, I've worked throughout my college career at WOUB Public Media, mainly reporting and producing spots and features for FM radio. Also at WOUB, I was in charge of The Outlet, a news magazine radio show airing on WOUB's AM station, where I also a radio host. I've reported and created daily spots and features at two public radio stations from around the country (Wyoming Public Radio and WYSO Public Radio), and I did election/Ohio statehouse politics coverage as a correspondent for WCPO-TV/WCPO Digital in the fall of 2016.

Feel free to reach out for questions, ideas, podcast recommendations or anything else.
