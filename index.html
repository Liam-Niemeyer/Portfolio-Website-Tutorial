Storytelling is my trade. On here, you'll find a collection of my journalistic work in radio, podcasting, print and more. I'm just now finishing up my senior year at Ohio University, with an aspiration to tell great stories online and on-air.

A quick summary of my experience: I've worked throughout my college career at WOUB Public Media, mainly in charge of a student-run news magazine radio show, The OUtlet, while also doing hosting on WOUB 1340 AM. I've reported and created daily spots and features at two public radio stations from around the country (Wyoming Public Radio and WYSO Public Radio), and I did election/Ohio statehouse politics coverage as a correspondent for WCPO-TV/WCPO Digital in the fall of 2016. Right now, I'm creating a narrative podcast series I'm planning on launching this upcoming spring.

Feel free to reach out for questions, ideas, podcast recommendations or anything else.
